COMPUTER AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Computer and Communication Engineering aims at providing a strong foundation for the students aspiring for a career requiring skills from computer science and computer communications with an emphasis on communication and computing. The curriculum is designed to provide flexibility for students to opt for minor specialization offered by the department and online courses by reputed international universities.Computer & communication engineering(CCE) is the mixture of two or more branches like computer science, electronics and telecommunication engineering where you will be learning Coding, Programming languages, Machine languages, Database management, , networks and protocols, VLSI systems (Very Large Scale Integrated circuits), Display engineering, image and speech processing, pattern recognition, robotics etc etc.

ORIGINATION OF CCE

Computer and Communication Engineering as a field began to take shape in india during the late 20th century.The exact origin can be traced to the establishment of engineering colleges and universities that started offering specialized courses in these areas around the 1980s and 1990s.The growth of engineering technology and the demand for skilled professionals in computer and communication engineering played a significant role in the development of this field in india.The development of communication engineering is tied to advancements in telecommunication technologies.

FOCUS

The course focuses on core subjects in computer science, communication networks, cloud computing, and big data analytics

CAREER PROSPECTUS

They would be employable in companies related to software development, computer communication-related industry, IOT based application industry, Sensor technology, machine learning, artificial intelligence, big data analysis, networking, and many more. In addition, they can pursue their higher education/research in the above areas at national or international universities.

DETAILED PREVIEW

Computer and Communication engineering is designed as a synergistic hybrid of both computing and communication technologies.

SALARY PACKAGE

Communication Engineer salary in India ranges between 2.7 lakhs to 20.1 lakhs with an average annual salary of 6.0 lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 102 latest salaries received from communucation engineers.
